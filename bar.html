About Our Bar & Restaurant

Located along the serene banks of Pawna Lake, just a short drive from Mumbai, Shantai Bar & Restaurant offers an exquisite dining experience with breathtaking lake views, lush greenery, and organic farm-to-table cuisine.

Our elegantly designed space features cozy seating, modern amenities including free Wi-Fi, and en-suite dining areas for ultimate comfort.

Whether you're seeking a romantic dinner, a lively bar experience, or live entertainment, Shantai Bar & Restaurant is your gateway to gastronomic pleasure with proximity to the scenic Konkan region.
